We are ORKID

Art - Music - Clothing

ORKID is a multidisciplinary art collective based in Limerick. United by our
passion for pushing creative boundaries, we promote collaboration across a variety of mediums.
ORKID brings together artists, designers, performers,and thinkers from diverse backgrounds,
each contributing their unique perspectives to create innovative and experimantal works.
Whether through visual art, digital experiences, music, performance, or installations, ORKID aims to
engage with our community, proposing safe spaces for conversations around art and culture.
ORKID has commitment to experimentation and social inclusivity, where creativity can flourish
and inspire others to explore new artistic possibilities.
