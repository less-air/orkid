We are orKid

Art - Music - Clothing

orKid is a multidisciplinary art collective based in Limerick,
united by our passion for pushing creative boundaries and fostering collaboration across a variety of mediums.
orKid brings together artists, designers, performers, and thinkers from diverse backgrounds,
each contributing their unique perspectives to create innovative and thought-provoking works.
Whether through visual art, digital experiences, music, performance, or installations,
we aim to engage with our community and spark meaningful conversations around art and culture.
orKid has commitment to experimentation and inclusivity, and strives to create spaces where creativity can flourish,
challenge conventions, and inspire others to explore new artistic possibilities.
